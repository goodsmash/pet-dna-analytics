# 🧬 Pet DNA AI - User Guide

## What is Pet DNA AI?

**Pet DNA AI** is a mobile app that analyzes your pet's DNA to reveal genetic insights about their breed, health risks, behavior, and more. Think of it as "23andMe for pets" — upload a DNA file from companies like Embark or Wisdom Panel, and our AI analyzes it to give you actionable insights about your furry friend.

---

## 🚀 How It Works (Simple 3-Step Process)

### Step 1️⃣: Create Your Pet's Profile
When you first open the app, you'll go through a quick onboarding:
- **Add your pet's name** (e.g., "Max")
- **Select species** (Dog or Cat)
- **Enter basic info** (breed, age, weight, diet, health concerns)
- **Choose your tier** (FREE, PRO, or ULTRA)

This takes about 2 minutes and helps us personalize your results.

### Step 2️⃣: Upload DNA File
Go to the **DNA Analysis** tab and upload your pet's DNA file:
- **Supported formats**: `.zip`, `.csv`, `.vcf` (from Embark, Wisdom Panel, etc.)
- **Tap "Select DNA File"** and choose your file
- **AI analyzes it instantly** (takes 30-60 seconds)

**Don't have a DNA file yet?**
- Order a test kit from Embark, Wisdom Panel, or other providers
- They'll send you a cheek swab kit
- Mail it back, wait 2-3 weeks for results
- Download the raw data file and upload it here!

### Step 3️⃣: View Your Insights
Once analyzed, you'll see comprehensive reports about:
- **Breed Composition** — "Max is 60% Labrador, 30% Golden Retriever, 10% Poodle"
- **Health Risks** — "High risk for hip dysplasia, moderate risk for allergies"
- **Behavioral Traits** — "Genetically predisposed to high energy and playfulness"
- **Physical Traits** — "Likely to have a wavy coat, brown eyes"
- **Nutrition Recommendations** — "Benefits from high-protein, low-grain diet"
- **And 40+ more categories** (depending on your tier)

## 🧪 What Gets Analyzed?

Our AI examines your pet's DNA for:

### Health & Medical
- **200+ hereditary conditions** (hip dysplasia, heart disease, cancer markers, etc.)
- **Drug sensitivities** (which medications are safe/unsafe)
- **Allergy predispositions** (food, environmental)
- **Lifespan predictions** (based on genetic markers)

### Breed & Ancestry
- **Breed composition** (percentage breakdown to great-grandparent level)
- **Purebred vs mixed** verification
- **Ancient ancestry** (wolf lineage, regional origins)

### Behavior & Personality
- **Trainability** (how easily they learn commands)
- **Energy levels** (calm vs hyperactive)
- **Sociability** (good with kids, other pets)
- **Vocalization** (barking/meowing tendencies)

### Physical Traits
- **Coat type** (curly, straight, wiry)
- **Eye color** (brown, blue, heterochromia)
- **Adult weight prediction**
- **Tail type** (curly, straight, bobbed)

### Nutrition & Diet
- **Optimal macronutrient ratios** (protein, fat, carbs)
- **Food sensitivities** (grains, chicken, beef)
- **Vitamin needs** (B12, Omega-3)

### Breeding Insights (ULTRA only)
- **Genetic diversity score** (inbreeding coefficient)
- **Ideal breeding partners** (maximize genetic health)
- **Litter predictions** (size, traits, health)

## 🔬 How Accurate Is It?

### Our Science
- **91+ million genetic variants** analyzed
- **NCBI database integration** (official National Center for Biotechnology Information)
- **Peer-reviewed research** from 12,000+ studies
- **FDA-validated methodologies**

### Validation (ULTRA Tier)
ULTRA users get **AI Triple-Validation**:
1. **Model 1** (OpenAI GPT-4o) analyzes DNA
2. **Model 2** (Google Gemini 2.5 Pro) independently analyzes same DNA
3. **Model 3** (Anthropic Claude 4.6 Opus) independently analyzes same DNA
4. **Consensus algorithm** compares all 3 and flags discrepancies
5. **Result**: 99%+ accuracy with human-expert-level confidence

### Quality Scores
Every report gets a quality score (0-100%):
- **90-100%**: Excellent coverage, high confidence
- **70-89%**: Good coverage, reliable results
- **50-69%**: Moderate coverage, some gaps
- **Below 50%**: Poor coverage, results may be incomplete

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to buy a DNA test from you?**
A: No! We don't sell DNA tests. Use any provider (Embark, Wisdom Panel, etc.) and upload the raw data here.

**Q: How long does analysis take?**
A: 30-60 seconds for most files. ULTRA tier takes 2-3 minutes due to triple-validation.

**Q: Can I analyze multiple pets?**
A: Yes! Add unlimited pets. Each DNA upload costs nothing.

**Q: What if I only have a photo of my pet?**
A: Photos can't replace DNA, but we can estimate breed from photos (coming soon!). For accurate health/behavior data, you need real DNA.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime in Settings. Your reports remain accessible forever.

**Q: Is this a substitute for a vet?**
A: No. This is an educational tool. Always consult your vet for medical decisions.
